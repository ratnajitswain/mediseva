About Us

Medyseva is a healthcare startup focusing on Telemedicine in Rural India. It seeks to become the ultimate healthcare partner of all citizens such that they can get access to high quality healthcare services with ease and at affordable prices.Medyseva bridges the gap between the doctors and patients through e-clinics having state-of-the-art technology, which cover the entire spectrum of services that are needed to keep one healthy - from having virtual consultations to procuring medicines, from booking diagnostic tests to getting surgeries, from digitizing medical records to buying the right insurance.

Medyseva has identified several problems faced by the people in small towns and villages, pertaining to healthcare, and seeks to solve most of them in the short run.

Problem: Though there are doctors available in rural areas, but they are mostly general physicians. There is a dearth of specialists.

Our Solution: Medyseva connects people in rural areas to specialists residing in urban areas via teleconferencing. In this way, people do not need to travel to far off places to get a medical treatment. Also, many people do not have much knowledge about who to consult and how to go about the process. Medyseva eliminates all such hassles and directly connects the people to excellent doctors in each field.

Services

Medyseva offers you every service you could possibly need in your journey to achieve great health and wellness. We are there with you at every step of the way.

Consultation

The primary service provided by Medyseva is online/ teleconsultation. Our goal is to make the best doctors and specialists available to everyone, irrespective of their location or resources. Our consultation fee is minimal so that this basic right to healthcare is availed by all.

01

Pharmacy

Medyseva has collaborated with local pharmacy stores at each location. In addition, these pharmacy stores are situated just next to the Medyseva centers. In this way, the patient gets all prescribed medicines immediately. And local entrepreneurs are also promoted.

02

Laboratory

LaboratorySimilarly, we have collaborated with the best laboratory chains in the country. The samples will be collected at our centers and they will be sent to the nearest lab for testing, after which the reports can also be collected from our centers. The patient does not need to deliver samples or collect reports himself.

03

Radiology

Our centers are located in rural areas where good radiology services are not available. This is where we come in. We have collaborations with the best radiology centers in the nearest cities. The patient will be guided throughout the entire process.

04

Ambulance

We provide ambulance service for the patients who come to us for consultations. Even if the patient wants to go to any other hospital of his/ her choice, he/ she can do so, and we will help by providing ambulance services.

05

Home Care Services

For patients who are advised bedrest or need to stay at home for their long recovery process, we provide trained nurses and other care-takers.
